Agent Details

Control plane

Botler

What they doReceives the human request, decides whether the work should stay in the main session or route to a specialist, keeps approval gates visible, and turns scattered work into durable reports.

How they workBotler works from the shared memory, run ledger, operating-mode contracts, and project artifacts. It does not treat chat as the source of truth when a file, receipt, route, or deploy can be checked.

ModelPrimary documented model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4 for Tier 0 direct sessions.

Long-term goalBecome the dependable front desk for the whole system: one command surface, clear routing, honest fallback disclosure, and no silent downgrade on household-critical or business-critical work.

Revenue and finance truth

Cash

What they doKeeps the money story honest: revenue movement, receivables, outreach proof, P&L gaps, and whether a project actually moved closer to cash.

How they workCash compares claims against ledgers, invoices, outreach trackers, payment receipts, broker/account receipts, and daily KPI artifacts before it lets a number become operator truth.

ModelRecent ledger model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4.

Long-term goalReach decision-grade financial reporting: clean daily cash position, booked revenue, overdue proofs, and automatic escalation when the source chain is missing.

Engineering repair and build execution

Dev

What they doHandles implementation, source inspection, deploy repair, runtime debugging, and the narrow code changes needed to unblock products.

How they workDev works against the real repo, the live route, the scheduler state, and the generated artifacts. It pairs with Red and Doc so fixes are checked and recorded instead of merely attempted.

ModelRecent ledger model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4.

Long-term goalBecome a reliable build lane that can take a scoped issue from diagnosis to verified deploy with receipts, while leaving unrelated work untouched.

F10.0RD growth and storefront operator

Jim

What they doRuns the music/storefront lane: content drafts, outbound opportunities, site health checks, catalog status, and approval-gated revenue actions.

How they workJim reads F10.0RD artifacts, content dashboards, draft packets, storefront checks, CRM/inbox trackers, and approval state before recommending a send, post, listing, or follow-up.

ModelRecent ledger model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4.

Long-term goalTurn F10.0RD into a measurable operating loop where content, outreach, storefront health, orders, and revenue all reconcile cleanly.

Runtime and transport layer

Hermes

What they doCarries runtime/orchestration context, agent communication, task handoffs, and switchboard-style execution without muddying Botler's family/business context.

How they workHermes owns operational heavy lifting: route messages, run bounded tasks, move state between agents, and raise `needs_decision` when the work requires Botler or Quan.

ModelDocumented as eligible for faster models; not allowed to make Tier 0 policy decisions.

Long-term goalBecome the clean transport layer for multi-agent work: reliable handoffs, clear job ownership, and no context bleed between specialists.

Cleanup, QA routing, and opportunity hygiene

Loki

What they doOwns cleanup, optimization, mode switching, TideFlow prospect hygiene, and recurring QA/opportunity movement loops.

How they workLoki reads trackers, stale dates, contact coverage, scheduler health, product QA reports, and operating-mode state to keep the machine and project lanes from drifting.

ModelRecent ledger model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4.

Long-term goalBecome the system's reliability and momentum layer: cleaner workspaces, healthier queues, sharper prospects, and mode-aware automation.

Trading discipline and market exposure

Impulse

What they doMonitors trading posture, open orders, positions, broker/source truth, paper signals, and whether live risk is allowed or should fail closed.

How they workImpulse refuses to book P&L or trade state without a durable receipt chain: balances, positions, orders, fills, fees, market data, and quote validation.

ModelRecent ledger model: openai-codex/gpt-5.4.

Long-term goalBecome a disciplined trading assistant with certified receipts, decision-grade exposure checks, and risk controls that fail closed when truth is incomplete.

Product QA and adversarial review

Red

What they doFinds broken flows, stale data, routing problems, misleading UI states, and product regressions before users run into them.

How they workRed combines static checks, local builds, smoke tests, route probes, and browser QA when tooling/session access exists. It records blockers instead of pretending a full pass happened.

ModelRecent ledger models: GPT-5.4 / GPT-5 Codex.

Long-term goalBecome the automatic quality gate for every public product: authenticated smoke tests, route checks, console review, stale-data detection, and deploy drift alerts.

Receipts, repairs, and artifact truth

Doc

What they doRepairs receipt chains, corrects stale scheduler/watchdog artifacts, writes clear status truth, and keeps operational records aligned with live state.

How they workDoc cross-checks run logs, jobs-state, repair tickets, ledgers, and report files, then patches the durable record without triggering public sends or risky external actions.

ModelRecent ledger models: gpt-5.5 and gpt-5.5/openai-codex.

Long-term goalBecome the audit layer for AgenticOS: every important run should leave a clean, current, verifiable trail.

Financial planning and strategy

Atlas

What they doSupports higher-level financial planning, portfolio strategy, debt elimination, wealth-building work, and supervision of research lanes.

How they workAtlas works from shared context, Scout research, financial recovery plans, and strategy notes, then turns market and household constraints into practical decisions.

ModelConfigured model: anthropic/claude-sonnet-4-5.

Long-term goalBecome a trustworthy strategic advisor with strong tool discipline, clear fallback disclosure, and durable plans that Cash can later verify against real numbers.

Research and opportunity discovery

Scout

What they doScans markets, pain points, product opportunities, trends, and expansion signals so Atlas, Botler, and product agents can make better calls.

How they workScout is optimized for fast research passes and usable summaries, with factual accuracy and cited source trails prioritized over novelty.

ModelConfigured model: google/gemini-2.5-flash.

Long-term goalBecome the first-pass research radar for new product wedges, market shifts, competitor signals, and customer pain worth turning into action.

Goal

What we aimed for

Move beyond one giant assistant by giving work to distinct agents with dedicated context, source files, tools, and escalation rules.

Approach

How we tried

The system uses Botler as the control plane, specialist agents for domains, Hermes-style runtime/switchboard patterns, context matrices, run ledgers, nightly dreams, and approval gates so important facts live in files instead of hidden chat state.

Outcome

Did it work?

Partially achieved and actively used. The structure can route, remember, report, and block unsafe action, but public packaging and some runtime identity cleanup still need owner decisions.

Still Unknown

Needs owner truth

  • Public packaging/pricing for AgenticOS is not documented.
  • Which pieces should become a reusable client-facing system need owner confirmation.
  • Hermes is documented as runtime/switchboard in architecture notes, but its current identity files mirror Jim and should be cleaned up before being presented as fully resolved.
